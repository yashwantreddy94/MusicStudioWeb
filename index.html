Manifesto

Founded in 2021 by genre-blending artist AARYA, The Elysium Collective is a forward-thinking music label born to break boundaries in the world of new wave Hip-Hop and R&B. Built on a vision of musical freedom, emotional honesty, and fearless creativity, the label serves as a sanctuary for artists pushing sonic and visual limits. What started as a personal mission quickly grew into a movement rooted in underground culture, elevated by future-facing sound. Soon after its launch, Canadian artist BANZ2FADED joined the fold—bringing his atmospheric production, dark-toned melodies, and sharp lyrical instincts. Their partnership expanded the Collective's reach globally, bridging borders through shared emotion and artistry.
